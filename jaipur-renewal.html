Jaipur Renewable Energy Solar Solution

Understanding Jaipur Renewable Energy Solar Solution

Client Overview

  • Client: Jaipur Renewable Energy Solar Solution
  • Industry: Residential Rooftop Solar
  • Service: Solar panel consultation, installation & subsidy assistance for homeowners in Jaipur

Objective

The client’s primary goal was to generate verified leads from homeowners interested in solar energy, while keeping the cost per lead low. The campaign was designed to:

  • Run only on Facebook Ads
  • Use instant lead forms, not landing pages
  • Automate follow-ups via WhatsApp to reduce manual effort

Our Strategy

We launched a full-funnel paid ad campaign with built-in automation:

1. Hyperlocal Targeting:

We focused only on Jaipur city and nearby suburbs, targeting homeowners likely to qualify for the PM Kusum Yojana subsidy.

2. Scroll-Stopping Creatives:

Messaging revolved around:

  • ₹95,000 Govt. Subsidy
  • Free Solar Consultation
  • Up to 80% Savings on Electricity Bills

3. Lead Form Ads:

We used Facebook’s native lead forms for instant submissions — no landing page required.

4. WhatsApp Automation Setup:

Every submitted lead received an automated WhatsApp message within 30 seconds — sharing solar benefits, documentation checklist, and scheduling link for consultation.

This helped warm leads immediately and reduce drop-off.

Results Delivered

  • 500+ Qualified Residential Leads
  • 16% Lead-to-Consultation Conversion Rate
  • Cost Per Lead: Only ₹30
  • 80%+ Leads Engaged via WhatsApp in First Hour
