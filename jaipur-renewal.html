Jaipur Renewable Energy Solar Solution

Understanding Jaipur Renewable Energy Solar Solution

Client Overview

  • Client: Jaipur Renewable Energy Solar Solution
  • Industry: Residential Rooftop Solar
  • Service:Solar panel consultation, installation & subsidy assistance for homeowners in Jaipur

Objective

The client’s primary goal was to generate verified leads from homeowners interested in solar energy, while keeping the cost per lead low. The campaign, executed by our expert Facebook Ads Agency in Jaipur, aimed to:

  • Run exclusively on Facebook Ads
  • Use instant lead forms (no landing pages)
  • Automate follow-ups via WhatsApp to minimize manual effort

Our Strategy

As a trusted Meta Ads Agency in Jaipur, we launched a full-funnel paid ad campaign with automation built in:

1. Hyperlocal Targeting:

Focused only on Jaipur city and nearby suburbs, targeting homeowners eligible for PM Kusum Yojana subsidy.

2. Scroll-Stopping Creatives:

Messaging revolved around:

  • ₹95,000 Govt. Subsidy
  • Free Solar Consultation
  • Up to 80% Savings on Electricity Bills

3. Lead Form Ads:

Used Facebook’s native lead forms to maximize submissions without a landing page.

4. WhatsApp Automation Setup:

Every lead received an automated WhatsApp message within 30 seconds, including:

  • Solar benefits overview
  • Documentation checklist
  • Scheduling link for free consultation

This helped nurture leads instantly and reduce drop-off.

Results Delivered

  • 500+ Qualified Residential Leads
  • 16% Lead-to-Consultation Conversion Rate
  • Cost Per Lead: Only ₹30
  • 80%+ Leads Engaged via WhatsApp in First Hour

Campaign Executed by Vijeta Renewable Solar Solution in collaboration with a top-performing Facebook Ads Agency in Jaipur.
