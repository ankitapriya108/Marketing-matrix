Balaji Coach Pvt. Ltd.

Case Study: Balaji Coach Pvt. Ltd.

Client Overview

  • Client: Balaji Coach Pvt. Ltd.
  • Industry: Tempo Traveller Customization, Vanity Van Fabrication, Bus Body Building
  • Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan
  • Target Market: Pan India — Tour operators, corporate buyers, hotel groups, event planners

Objective

Balaji Coach partnered with Marketing Matrix to:

  • Launch a national-level performance campaign
  • Attract bulk buyers & serious inquiries for vehicle customization
  • Scale lead generation through both Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and Google Ads
  • Use multimedia ads and landing pages to showcase real product value

Our Strategy

To generate high-volume, high-quality leads from across India, we implemented a full-stack marketing funnel:

1. Facebook & Instagram Campaigns (Meta Ads):

We targeted travel agencies, hospitality businesses, corporates, and event planners using:

  • High-impact graphics & video ads showing before-after customization
  • Region-wise campaigns in Hindi + English
  • Direct WhatsApp lead forms for instant query handling

Results:

4,693+ Verified Leads in 4 Months (via Meta Ads)

2. Google Search Campaigns:

We ran intent-based Google Ads targeting high-converting keywords like:

“Tempo Traveller body builder,” “vanity van manufacturers in India,” “bus body customization Jaipur” etc.

Results:

300+ Qualified Leads via Google Ads (across 6 major states)

3. Landing Pages & Funnel Design

  • Designed fast-loading, mobile-friendly landing pages with embedded forms & CTA buttons
  • Integrated WhatsApp for lead capture + instant communication
  • Product photos, testimonials, and work videos used to increase trust

Results Summary

  • 4,693+ Leads via Facebook & Instagram Ads
  • 300+ Leads via Google Search
  • Pan India Visibility in 20+ States
  • Industries Engaged: Travel, Events, Government Contracts, Hospitality, Corporates
  • 60% Leads Engaged via WhatsApp in 15 Minutes
