Car Spa & Shine

Case Study: Car Spa & Shine

Client Overview

  • Client: Car Spa & Shine
  • Location: 200 Feet Bypass, Jaipur
  • Industry: Automotive Detailing & Car Cleaning Services
  • Services: Car Spa, Interior Cleaning, Polishing, Ceramic Coating

Objective

Car Spa & Shine partnered with us to:

  • Generate high-intent local leads using Meta Ads
  • Automate bookings and inquiries through WhatsApp
  • Scale monthly service volumes without increasing staff load
  • Build a funnel that works on autopilot — from ad click to confirmed slot

Our Strategy

We built a full-performance funnel focused on hyperlocal lead capture and fast WhatsApp automation:

1. Meta Ads Campaign (Facebook + Instagram)

  • Ran location-specific campaigns around 200 Feet Bypass, Mansarovar, and Ajmer Road
  • Creatives showed real results: before-after photos, detailing in action, reviews
  • Offers like “Monsoon Cleaning ₹499” & “Ceramic Coating with Pickup” used to trigger response

Ad Types:

Static Image | Video Reels | Click-to-WhatsApp

2. WhatsApp Automation Funnel

Click-to-WhatsApp ads opened a smart chatbot that:

  • Shared service menus
  • Sent location pins + Google Maps link
  • Offered booking time slots
  • Escalated hot leads to human agent

3. Retargeting Ads for Non-Converters

  • Re-engaged website/WhatsApp viewers who didn’t book
  • Offered limited-time discounts or loyalty coupons

Results Summary

  • 4,000+ Verified Leads in Local Jaipur Area
  • Cost-Efficient, Auto-Filtered Inquiries
  • 80% WhatsApp Engagement in First 5 Minutes
  • Spike in Repeat Customers via Funnel Follow-Ups
